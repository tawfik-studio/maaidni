About me


I’m Taoufik MAAIDNI, I got my PhD in 2012 from Sidi Mohamed Ben Abdellah University, Fez, Morocco. Currently I’m a professor at the department of computer science, University of Sultan Moulay Slimane, Beni Mellal, Morocco.

I work in a multidisciplinary environment involving machine learning, educational data mining (EDM), semantic web, knowledge management, Web Personalization and Recommender Systems, E-learning and semantic E-learning. My research focus is to study those research fields to come up with solutions to various real-world problems. I published several research papers at international journals and conferences. I served as a member of the technical committees of several international conferences and workshops.

Since 2013, I have been a member of the reading committee & program of several conferences and journals : Journal of Big Data, Journal of e-Learning and Knowledge Society Je-LKS, International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Computer Vision ISCV’2020, The 3rd International Conference on Information Technology & Electrical Engineering ITee’19, The 4th International Conference On Big Data and Internet of Things BDIoT’19, International Conference On Embedded Systems And Artificial Intelligence ESAI’19, The international conference on WIreless Technologies, embedded and intelligent Systems WITS-2019 , International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Computer Vision 2018 ISCV2018, The 4th IEEE Conference on Information and Multimedia Processing IEEE IMP2018, The 3rd International Conference on Big Data, Cloud and Applications BDCA’18, The 2nd International Conference on Big Data, Cloud and Applications BDCA’17.

Since 2007, I have taught computer science in several departments and several training courses (Initial, Master, engineer and professional) in several universities (public and private): Java EE Frameworks (ESTA-OFPPT), project management (Sup’management), advanced web development and Objective-C (FST-Settat), C++, UML, Algorithm (FP-Khouribga). For more information, please refer to my CV page.

Since 2014, I have been the Education Coordinator of the Mathematics and computer sciences branch, and the responsible for many modules: object-oriented programming, Unified Modelling Language (UML), algorithms and data structures, operating systems, computer architecture, computer databases, …

Beside teaching, I participated in many activities in the Polydisciplinary faculty of Khouribga, such as interviewing candidates and preparing recruiting competitions for new specialized technicians in IT development and IT networks.
